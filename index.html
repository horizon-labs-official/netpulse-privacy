Privacy Policy

We don't collect any personal data.
Feel free and safe to use NetPulse. Your personal information always stays on your device. The app may display ads to support free access.

01 — Data Collection

What We Don't Collect

NetPulse is built on a simple principle: your data belongs to you. We collect absolutely nothing personal.

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No location data stored or transmitted
  • No usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • No network data sent to our servers
  • No account registration required
  • No cookies or cross-app tracking
02 — How The App Works

What NetPulse Does

Network Reading
NetPulse reads your WiFi name, signal strength and speed purely to display it on your screen. This data is never stored or transmitted anywhere.
Location Permission
Android requires location permission to read your WiFi network name (SSID). We use this solely to display your network name — nothing else. No location coordinates are ever accessed or stored.
Settings Storage
Your app preferences (toggles, DNS selection) are saved locally on your device using standard Android storage. This data never leaves your device.
DNS Optimization
When you switch DNS to Cloudflare (1.1.1.1) or another provider, your queries go directly to that provider — not through us. We have no visibility into your DNS activity.
03 — App Permissions

Why We Need Permissions

NetPulse requests only the permissions strictly necessary for its features:

  • INTERNET — to measure ping latency to DNS servers
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE — to read WiFi signal strength and connection info
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION — required by Android to read WiFi network name only
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — to detect connection type (WiFi / Mobile Data)
  • KILL_BACKGROUND_PROCESSES — to throttle background apps during boost
  • WAKE_LOCK — to keep optimization running while screen is on
  • VIBRATE — for haptic feedback on boost button tap
04 — Advertising

Ads & Third-Party Services

NetPulse is free to use. To support continued development, the app may display advertisements served by third-party ad networks.

About Ads
Ad networks may collect certain non-personal, anonymized data (such as device type, general location, or ad interaction data) for ad targeting and measurement purposes. This is governed by each ad network's own privacy policy. We do not share your personal data with advertisers, and we do not sell your data.

The app also makes the following direct network requests:

  • Ping requests to 1.1.1.1 (Cloudflare) to measure latency — no personal data attached
  • Google Fonts from fonts.googleapis.com for app typography
05 — Children's Privacy

Children's Privacy

NetPulse does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.
